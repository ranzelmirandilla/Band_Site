Origins of Mundo (Summer 2019)

After many years of traveling and songwriting - Mundo was formed in 2019 and is a mix of rock and alternative music. Our band is constantly creating music that is enjoyed by people in many countries throughout the world. We have taken our inspirations from bands such as Maroon 5, Milky Chance. If you are looking to listen to something with a new unique vibe, then check us out and listen to one of our new videos on our YouTube channel.

Song Selection (July 2019)

Mundo has been writing and collaborators with other musicians for almost a decade. The music creation process can be a long journey at times, but we are always in the studio editing to make the latest release something that our fans will be psyched about.

East Coast Tour (November 2019 - February 2020)

We are kicking off our first tour in beautiful Upstate New York. We have eight shows lined up from December 2019- February 2020, so be sure to check our line-up of local venues in the Capital Region.
